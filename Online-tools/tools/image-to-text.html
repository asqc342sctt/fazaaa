What is Image to Text (OCR)?

OCR (Optical Character Recognition) is the technology that converts text within images into editable, searchable text. This tool runs entirely in your browser using modern WebAssembly-based OCR, so your files never leave your device.

How it Works

  1. Upload or drag & drop an image with text.
  2. Our in-browser OCR engine analyzes the image.
  3. You get selectable, copyable text you can edit or download as TXT.

Main Features

  • Private by design: Processing happens locally in your browser.
  • Fast: Optimized OCR engine for quick results.
  • Simple: One-click extraction with a clean interface.
  • Download: Save results as a TXT file instantly.

Supported Formats

JPG, PNG, WEBP, BMP, and TIFF images are supported.

Great For

  • Notes, receipts, invoices, and printed documents
  • Screenshots of text (web pages, chats, slides)
  • Quick digitization of small text blocks

Privacy

Your images are never uploaded to a server. All processing is performed locally in your browser for maximum privacy and security.

FAQ

Q: Does it support handwriting?
A: Handwriting recognition quality varies and is not guaranteed. Printed text works best.

Q: Can I use it offline?
A: After the first load, many browsers cache the OCR engine. Subsequent uses may work with limited connectivity.
